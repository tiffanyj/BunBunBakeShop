Classic Cinnamon Roll

At Bun Bun Bake Shop, we use a special family recipe to make our classic cinnamon rolls. This recipe has been passed down for four generations. People come into the shop every day just to get their hands on one of these classic cinnamon rolls. They're perfectly soft and fluffy. Our frosting is homemade as well, with a touch of honey and brown sugar.
